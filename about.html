Background

Jhene Aiko Efuru Chilombo is an American artist from Los Angeles, California.
Her stage name, Jhene Aiko stems from her first and middle name. She was born on March
16th, 1988. She's been actively creating music since 2002, but it wasn't until the release
of her mixtape, Sailing Souls (2011), followed by her debut album,
Sail Out (2013), when her career really took off. She's found great success
with her eclectic style of Alternative R&B, Neo Soul, and Hip Hop. She is signed to Atrium
Records through Def Jam. During her break out year, (2013), she headlined for a number of
shows for huge artists including Nas, Drake, and Lauryn Hill. Her next album,
Souled Out (2014), which debuted at number three on the Billboard top 200 US charts.

In 2015, Jhene Aiko was nominated for three Grammy awards, the same year she
joined J.Cole on tour. In 2016, Jhene Aiko released an album, Twenty88, with her
current significant other Big Sean. She also released mulitple singles during this year.
In 2017, her sophomore album Trip, was released and recieved rave reviews. This album
was released with a 23 minute autobiographical film, aptly named with the same title.
