Background

Jhene Aiko Efuru Chilombo is an American artist from Los Angeles, California.
Her stage name, Jhene Aiko stems from her first and middle name. She was born on March
16th, 1988. She's been actively creating music since 2002, but it wasn't until the release
of her mixtape, Sailing Souls (2011), followed by her debut album, Sail Out (2012),
when she really took off.

The band found even further success with their third album, White Pony,

is the group's most commercially successful single, and the album
track "Elite" won a Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance. White
Pony was the band's first of three albums to be certified platinum
in the United States.
